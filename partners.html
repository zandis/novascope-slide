Foundry Partner

UMC — United Microelectronics Corporation

World’s #3 dedicated semiconductor foundry.

Novascope is a direct customer of UMC, ordering production lots of 25 eight-inch wafers (~50,000 chips per lot) on the 0.18µm CMOS process node. These are dedicated production runs with full wafer acceptance testing, not shuttle prototypes.

Significance: Being a direct UMC customer validates the production-readiness of our chip designs. UMC’s 0.18µm process is a mature, cost-effective node appropriate for mixed-signal biosensor design — not too advanced (wasteful for analog biosensing) and mature enough for high-yield production.
