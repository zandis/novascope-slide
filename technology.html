Semiconductor DNA

First-in-Class BioFabless Design House

Inspired by the fabless semiconductor paradigm, Novascope develops and owns 100% of its silicon IP, focusing on chip design excellence. As a direct customer of UMC — Taiwan’s premier semiconductor foundry — we leverage world-class CMOS manufacturing infrastructure at the 0.18µm process node.

Our analogue ASIC is built on extended-gate FET (EG-FET) architecture, purpose-designed for ultra-sensitive biomarker detection at low concentrations and high-performance molecular diagnostics in real-world clinical matrices.

Semiconductor IC Design Model Applied to IVD

Different from many IC design houses that rely heavily on standard IP blocks and automated EDA tool flows, we have developed our own silicon IP from first principles. This allows us to preserve full design control, ensure maximum performance tuning for biosensing, and maintain zero external IP licensing — zero royalties.

Integrated Platform

Six Pillars of the BioSemiconductor Platform

Each pillar represents an independently difficult capability. Together, they form a vertically integrated ecosystem that competitors cannot replicate without years of parallel development.

NovaCHIP™
Our analogue ASIC, built on EG-FET architecture at UMC. 6 generations, 3 chip families. 100% in-house silicon IP — zero external IP, zero royalties. Each 3×3mm die contains billions of probe sensor sites. Production lots of 25 wafers (~50K chips/lot).
NovaLINK™
Patented surface chemistry platform. Probes embedded directly into the engineered chip surface. Achieves dual function: high-affinity probe attachment AND antifouling against the ~7,000 proteins in human blood. Protected as trade secret.
NovaBIO™
77 antibody clones across 15+ target families (p-Tau 181/217/231, NfL, GFAP, Aβ 42/40, TDP-43, α-Synuclein, S100B, UCH-L1). ZNA probes for pathogen detection. VHH nanobodies (~15 kDa vs ~150 kDa IgG). 6 products currently for sale.
NovaKIT™
Complete IVD hardware: NovaReader with ARM Cortex-M3 MCU, custom front-end amplifier for femtoampere-level measurement. ~200 components. Three form factors: Bamboo (64 tests), Lab Automation (1–3 tests), POCT Reader (single test, portable).
NovaAI™
Signal intelligence at femtogram-per-milliliter sensitivity. Proprietary algorithms analyze binding curve dynamics, subtract systematic noise, compensate for chip-to-chip variation, and classify results with confidence scores. Co-optimized with chip and electronics.
NovaCRO™
Proprietary biorepository from Chang Gung Memorial Hospital. 700+ bacterial isolates across 394 species from ~30 years. 206 WGS-ready, 494 DNA-extracted. PET-validated AD samples. Probes designed from real clinical genomic data, not reference databases.

Workflow

The BioFET Advantage: From Silicon to Signals

A simple and seamless diagnostic workflow. Each step is designed to minimize complexity while maximizing performance.

1

Bare Chip

Precision fabricated at UMC. Each wafer undergoes rigorous electrical testing and quality verification. ~50,000 chips per production lot.

2

Functional Biochip

Proprietary bioprobe design and surface coating via NovaLINK™. Transforms precision silicon die into a functional biochip with embedded probes.

3

Apply Sample

Any molecule that carries electric charges — DNA, RNA, protein, small molecules. The platform is molecule-agnostic.

4

Bio-Sensing

Target molecule binds to bioprobe, altering electric charge distribution at chip surface. Detected immediately by the underlying FET sensor architecture.

5

Signal Reading

Electrical signal digitized directly on-chip. Results generated in real time, enabling rapid clinical decisions. Digitally native from the start.

6

AI Analytics

NovaAI™ signal intelligence. Aggregated signal data, pathogen strain intelligence, and clinical context for advanced decision support (future expansion).

Full-Stack Biochemistry

Biochemistry Built into Silicon

NovaBIO™ — The Biological Toolkit

Microfluidics-based, fast-tracked sample and buffer system designed to streamline sample preparation and stabilize complex biological inputs. Optimizes fluid dynamics at the microscale for reproducibility and reduces turnaround time.

High-Purity Antibodies: A Strategic Asset

77 antibody clones developed entirely in-house — from animal immunization through hybridoma screening, gene sequencing, recombinant expression, and purification to pharmaceutical-grade specifications (HPLC-SEC purity >97%, SDS-PAGE >95%, endotoxin 0–1 EU/mg).

VHH nanobodies (~15 kDa vs ~150 kDa for conventional IgG): positions binding site closer to sensing surface for stronger signals. IgG mAbs produced via CHO cell expression — the gold standard for therapeutic-grade biologics.

Ready for Monetization: 6 products currently for sale, validated through ELISA, Western blot, IHC, immunofluorescence, and SPR kinetics.

High-purity antibodies — both a core platform enabler and standalone revenue asset

NovaLINK™ — Surface Chemistry

Patented surface chemistry platform where probes are embedded directly within the engineered surface coating. Unlike conventional assays relying on secondary labels or amplification, NovaLINK provides stable high-affinity binding while preserving electrical sensitivity.

Dual function: Specificity (probe attachment) + antifouling (rejecting ~7,000 proteins and billions of cells in human blood). On a direct electrical detection platform, every nonspecifically bound molecule generates a real signal — the antifouling must be essentially perfect.

Protected as trade secret.

Data-Driven Probe Design

WGS (Whole-Genome Sequencing) library construction powers rapid assay development. 700+ bacterial isolates from Chang Gung Memorial Hospital — nearly 3 decades of clinical strains. 206 isolates WGS-ready, 494 prepared for DNA extraction.

Nucleic acid probes designed from real clinical genomic data, not reference databases. ZNA (Zip Nucleic Acids) — charge-neutral probes that minimize electrical background noise on the FET chip.
