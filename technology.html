Chapter 1 — NovaCHIP

The Chip

Inspired by the fabless semiconductor paradigm, Novascope develops and owns 100% of its silicon IP. As a direct customer of UMC — Taiwan’s premier semiconductor foundry — we leverage world-class CMOS manufacturing at the 0.18µm process node.

Our analogue ASIC is built on extended-gate FET (EG-FET) architecture, purpose-designed for ultra-sensitive biomarker detection. Unlike IC design houses that rely on standard IP blocks, we developed our silicon IP from first principles — zero external IP, zero royalties.

Novascope biochip dies — designed in-house, fabricated at UMC

Three Chip Families, Six Generations

NS01C

FET-Based Biosensing

Single-FET sensor with intrinsic control & multiplexing. 3×3mm die, up to 8 targets. All current pipeline products built on NS01C.

NS02A

Array Sensor Architecture

High-density array for scalable multiplexing across large biomarker panels. Designed for AMR profiling and high-throughput screening.

NS03A

Photoelectric Sensor

System-on-chip integrating FET sensors with photodiode elements, combining electrical and optical sensing on a single die.

Chapter 2 — NovaLINK + NovaBIO

The Chemistry

NovaLINK™ — Surface Chemistry

Patented surface chemistry where probes are embedded directly within the engineered surface coating. Provides stable high-affinity binding while preserving electrical sensitivity.

Dual function: Specificity (probe attachment) + antifouling (rejecting ~7,000 proteins and billions of cells in human blood). On a direct electrical detection platform, every nonspecifically bound molecule generates a real signal — the antifouling must be essentially perfect.

Protected as trade secret.

NovaLINK surface chemistry and sample preparation

NovaBIO™ — The Biological Toolkit

77 antibody clones developed entirely in-house — from immunization through hybridoma screening, gene sequencing, recombinant expression, and purification to pharmaceutical-grade specs (HPLC-SEC purity >97%, endotoxin 0–1 EU/mg).

VHH nanobodies (~15 kDa vs ~150 kDa IgG) position binding sites closer to the sensing surface for stronger signals. IgG mAbs produced via CHO cell expression.

6 products currently for sale — validated through ELISA, Western blot, IHC, immunofluorescence, and SPR kinetics.

High-purity antibodies — platform enabler and revenue asset

WGS library from Chang Gung Memorial Hospital: 700+ bacterial isolates across 394 species from ~30 years of clinical strains. 206 WGS-ready, 494 DNA-extracted.

Nucleic acid probes designed from real clinical genomic data, not reference databases. ZNA (Zip Nucleic Acids) — charge-neutral probes that minimize electrical background noise on the FET chip.
