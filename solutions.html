Go-to-Market

Market Entry Strategy

PHASE 1 — INITIAL TARGET

Hospital Central Labs

ICUs & ERs where <1 hour, culture-free testing delivers highest clinical and economic value. Blood samples for sepsis detection.

PHASE 2 — DEPLOYMENT

Pilot Programs & Reagent Rental

Minimize adoption barriers with reagent rental agreements. Razor-and-blade model: instrument placement + per-test reagent revenue.

PHASE 3 — EXPANSION

UTI & Community Settings

UTI testing expands addressable market to GP offices and community clinics. Urine samples for urinary tract infection panel.
