Our Story

Where Silicon Meets Biology

Novascope BioChips is a full-stack silicon engineering company combining IC design, biochemistry, surface chemistry, reader hardware, and AI analytics into a single, vertically integrated diagnostic platform.

Every biomolecule carries an electric charge. A field-effect transistor detects charge changes with extraordinary precision. FETs are the most manufactured, most reliable, most scalable objects in human history.

We chose diagnostics as our first vertical — not our only one.

From semiconductor wafer to clinical diagnostic — built entirely in-house
