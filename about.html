Our Mission

Building the semiconductor platform that transforms diagnostics. We are not an IVD company that happens to use chips. We are an IC design house that brings full-stack silicon engineering to the most pressing problems in clinical diagnostics.

Novascope BioChips combines deep IC design expertise with vertical integration across biochemistry, surface chemistry, reader hardware, and AI-driven analytics — all on a single semiconductor platform.

James Wu, PhD

Joyce Chen

Surface chemistry and antibody engineering lead at Novascope. Milestones

Our Journey

From founding to Series B — key milestones that define our trajectory.

2022
Company Founded
Novascope BioChips established with the vision of applying IC design principles to in-vitro diagnostics. Initial chip architecture and design work commenced.
2023
First Publication & Tape-Out
Ciou et al. published in Biosensors & Bioelectronics, establishing scientific credibility. NS01 chip completed first tape-out, moving from design to physical silicon.
2024
Series A & Foundry Partnership
Closed $13.5M Series A financing. Established UMC foundry partnership for scalable semiconductor manufacturing. Achieved ISO 13485 quality management system certification.
2025 · Q1–Q2
Verification & Panel Expansion
Third-party verification completed successfully. E. coli single-chip assay finalized for production. AMR (antimicrobial resistance) panel design initiated, expanding the clinical pipeline.
2025 · Q3–Q4
Recognition & Validation
Selected as NBRP DEMO DAY Top 7. BLOT platform completed external validation. SD-Lab technology transfer executed. Angelica Reader prototype achieved hardware milestone.
2026
Series B & Regulatory Acceleration
Closed $8.9M Series B ($22.5M cumulative). Launched dual-track regulatory strategy for parallel US and international market access. Completed FDA Establishment Registration.
